Senior Benefits Finder

Every year, billions in benefits that could lower an aging parent's cost of food, medicine, and Medicare go unclaimed. Solantis helps your family find what they qualify for.

According to the National Council on Aging, more than 9 million older adults miss out on an estimated $58 billion in benefits each year — usually because of awareness and paperwork, not eligibility. Solantis's benefits finder helps you spot the programs worth looking into so that money doesn't stay on the table.

What the benefits finder surfaces

  • Medicare Savings Programs — help paying Medicare premiums and costs
  • Extra Help (Low-Income Subsidy) — lower prescription drug costs
  • SNAP — food assistance many eligible seniors never claim
  • Local and community resources — utilities help, transportation, and more

How it works

Because Solantis already knows the basics of who you're caring for, it helps point you toward relevant programs in the same place you manage medications and care — so checking benefits isn't a separate, forgotten task. Final eligibility is always confirmed through the official program; Solantis helps you find what to pursue.
