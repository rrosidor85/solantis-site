Solantis vs MyTherapy

MyTherapy is a well-loved free app for tracking your own medications and health. Solantis is built for the family caring for someone else. Here's how to tell which fits you.

MyTherapy has earned a strong reputation as a free medication reminder and health-tracking app, centered on the person managing their own medications. Solantis takes a different starting point: the adult child and family coordinating an aging parent's care. Neither is "better" — they're built for different jobs.

The short version

Tracking your own pills, symptoms, and measurements? MyTherapy is excellent. Coordinating a parent's care across siblings — with benefits and emergency info in the mix? That's what Solantis is designed for.

Frequently asked questions

What's the main difference between Solantis and MyTherapy?

MyTherapy is focused on the individual managing their own medications and health. Solantis is built for families managing an aging parent, adding a shared care team, benefits discovery, and emergency information.

Are both apps free?

Both offer free use. The difference is fit: MyTherapy centers on the individual user, while Solantis is designed for a caregiver and family. Check each app's current details before deciding.

Which is better for caring for a parent?

If multiple family members are involved and you want benefits and emergency info in one place, Solantis is built for that. For tracking your own medications and health, MyTherapy is a strong choice.
