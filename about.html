About Solantis

Solantis is a caregiver-first senior care app for families helping aging parents — built by someone who has lived the worry of caring from a distance.

Built by a family caregiver

Solantis was founded by Robinson Rosidor, a family caregiver who built the app out of his own experience.

"I built Solantis because I've been where you are — trying to keep track of Mom's medications from across the state, worrying if she remembered her evening dose, not knowing what benefits she qualified for. No family should have to figure this out alone."

That perspective shapes every decision: Solantis is designed for the adult child and the family around them, not just the individual taking the medication.

What Solantis is — and what it isn't

Solantis helps families organize and coordinate an aging parent's care: medication reminders and missed-dose alerts, vitals tracking, a shared family care team, senior benefits discovery, and emergency medical information.

Just as importantly, Solantis is not a medical provider. It does not diagnose, treat, prevent, or cure any condition, and it is not a substitute for a doctor, pharmacist, nurse, or emergency service. It helps you stay informed and have better conversations with the professionals who care for your loved one.

A note on our name: Solantis (the caregiver app at solantis.health) is unrelated to similarly spelled company names in staffing or automotive industries. We are an independent senior-care app.

How we approach health information

Caregiving content is high-stakes, so we hold it to a high standard:

  • Authoritative sources. Our guides cite organizations such as AARP, the CDC, AHRQ, the National Council on Aging, and Medicare.gov.
  • Clear dates. Articles show when they were last updated.
  • Reviewed by the Solantis Care Team for accuracy and safe, non-clinical wording.
  • Medically safe language. We say "helps," "supports," and "flags potential issues" — never that the app replaces professional care.

We are actively expanding our review process, including clinical review of medical-adjacent content, and welcome input from pharmacists and clinicians who care about caregiver safety.

Our commitments to your family

  • Privacy-minded. Solantis is HIPAA-mindful, with data encrypted in transit and at rest, and you control who on the care team can see what.
  • Your data, your choice. You can delete your account and data at any time.
  • Transparent. Read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service anytime.
  • Accessible. Free to start, with no credit card and no medication limit.

Important disclaimers

Medical: Solantis provides organizational and informational tools, not medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about medications, symptoms, and health decisions.

AI features: Solantis may use automated, AI-assisted insights to highlight patterns for your awareness. These are not a medical diagnosis and should be reviewed with a professional.

Emergency: Solantis is not an emergency service. In a medical emergency, call 911 (or your local emergency number) immediately.
