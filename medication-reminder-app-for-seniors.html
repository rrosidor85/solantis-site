Medication Reminder App for Seniors

A free medication reminder app built for older adults — and the families who help them — so a dose is never missed without someone knowing.

For a parent managing five or more prescriptions, a simple alarm isn't enough. Solantis helps your whole family stay on top of medications together: clear reminders for your parent, and a heads-up for you when something is missed. It's free to start, with no limit on the number of medications.

Reminders that reach the right person

Solantis sends clear, scheduled reminders for each medication. Because schedules vary, you can set different times for different medications and anchor doses to daily routines like meals. The older adult sees a simple prompt; the caregiver gets visibility without hovering.

Missed-dose alerts for the whole care team

This is what turns a reminder into a safety net. When a scheduled dose isn't marked as taken, Solantis can alert the care team so a caregiver can follow up the same day — not discover it later. Everyone you invite sees the same up-to-date medication log.

Refill tracking so prescriptions never lapse

A missed refill is a missed dose. Solantis helps you track when each medication is running low so you can request refills in time — especially important for critical, ongoing prescriptions.

Built-in interaction awareness

As you add medications and supplements, Solantis flags potential interactions to review with your pharmacist or doctor. It helps you have a more informed conversation — it does not replace professional medical judgment.

Frequently asked questions

Is Solantis a free medication reminder app?

Yes. Solantis is free to start with no limit on the number of medications you can track, on both iOS and Android. No credit card is required.

Will I be alerted if my parent misses a dose?

Yes. When a scheduled dose isn't marked as taken, Solantis can alert the care team so a caregiver can follow up — turning a simple reminder into a shared safety net.

Is it easy enough for an older parent to use?

Solantis is designed so a caregiver can manage everything while the older adult simply responds to clear reminders. The interface uses large, readable text.

Does Solantis replace a pharmacist or doctor?

No. Solantis helps families organize and remember medications and flags potential interactions to discuss with a professional. It does not provide medical advice and is not a substitute for a doctor or pharmacist.
