FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Solantis

Is Solantis really free?

Yes, Solantis is completely free to download and use on both iOS and Android. There is no credit card required and no hidden fees. Our goal is to make elder care coordination accessible to every family.

How does the medication reminder system work?

Solantis sends smart medication reminders based on your loved one's prescription schedule. If a dose is missed, the app alerts your entire care team within 30 minutes. It also checks for drug interactions and tracks refill schedules so nothing falls through the cracks.

Can multiple family members use Solantis to coordinate care?

Absolutely. Solantis is designed as a family care coordination app. You can invite siblings, nurses, home health aides, or any caregiver with role-based access. Everyone sees the same real-time updates — medication logs, vital signs, tasks, and notes.

What kind of health insights does the AI provide?

Solantis uses AI-powered pattern detection to analyze medication adherence and vital sign trends over time. It can spot patterns like consistently missed evening doses, rising blood pressure trends, or potential drug interactions — and alerts your care team before small issues become emergencies.

What is the Emergency Mode?

Emergency Mode (also called "Break Glass") gives first responders instant access to your loved one's critical care information — current medications, allergies, medical conditions, and emergency contacts — with a single tap. No login required. It's designed for scenarios where every second counts.

How does Solantis help find senior benefits and discounts?

Solantis uses your location to discover nearby senior discounts, government assistance programs like Medicare and SNAP, housing assistance, and local community resources. The average household leaves over $7,200 in senior benefits unclaimed each year — Solantis helps you find what your family qualifies for.
