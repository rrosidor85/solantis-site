Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Solantis ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Solantis: Senior Care & Safety (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and phone number. We use this to create your account, enable family sharing features ("Care Team"), and facilitate housing inquiries you initiate.
  • Health & Medical Data: Medication names, dosages, schedules, adherence history, and vital signs (e.g., blood pressure, weight). This data is used solely to provide medication tracking and safety alerts.
  • Location Data: We collect precise location data to help you find nearby senior living facilities, pharmacies, and community resources.
  • User Content: Photos or documents you upload to the "Document Vault" (e.g., insurance cards, medical directives).
  • Sensitive Information: We process health data, which is considered sensitive personal information. We do so only for App Functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To track medications, send reminders, and sync data across your Care Team.
  • Product Personalization: To display relevant senior resources and safety alerts based on your profile.
  • Lead Generation (User-Initiated): If you submit a "Check Availability" form for a senior living facility, we forward your name and phone number to our partners (e.g., A Place for Mom) specifically to fulfill your request.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell your personal data to third-party advertisers for tracking purposes.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services to operate the App, including Supabase (database hosting) and RevenueCat (subscription processing).
  • Affiliate Partners: We share your contact information with housing partners (like A Place for Mom) only when you explicitly fill out a lead form to request information.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by:

  • Going to Profile > App Settings > Delete Account within the App, or
  • Contacting us at the email below.

For step-by-step account deletion instructions, see our Account Deletion page.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, to protect your personal and health information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults caring for seniors. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: robinsonrosidor@gmail.com.
