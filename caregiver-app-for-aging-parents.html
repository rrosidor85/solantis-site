Caregiver App for Aging Parents

Everything you need to help care for an aging parent — medications, health, family coordination, benefits, and emergency information — in one free app.

Caring for an aging parent means juggling prescriptions, doctor visits, daily check-ins, and a family that's often spread across cities. Solantis brings it all into one place so nothing slips and no one carries it alone. It was built by a caregiver who lived it.

Medication safety

Reminders, missed-dose alerts to the care team, refill tracking, and interaction awareness help your family stay on top of medications together. See the medication reminder features →

Vitals & health tracking

Log blood pressure, glucose, weight, and more, and share clean summaries with doctors. Solantis helps you spot trends worth a conversation — it doesn't diagnose or treat.

A shared family care team

Invite siblings, a spouse, nurses, or aides with role-based access. Everyone sees the same medication list, tasks, and notes in real time, so you stop re-explaining and second-guessing. Explore care coordination →

Benefits discovery

Surface senior programs your family may be missing — Medicare Savings, SNAP, prescription help, and local resources. Read the senior benefits guide →

Emergency medical information

Keep a current, shareable summary of medications, allergies, and emergency contacts so first responders have what they need fast. It supports — it never replaces — emergency services.

Frequently asked questions

What does the Solantis caregiver app do?

It helps families caring for aging parents keep medications, health vitals, tasks, benefits, and emergency information in one shared place, with reminders and a care team everyone can see.

Is the caregiver app free?

Yes, Solantis is free to start on iOS and Android, with no credit card required and no limit on the number of medications you can track.

Can my whole family use it together?

Yes. Invite siblings, a spouse, nurses, or home aides to a shared care team with role-based access, so everyone works from the same up-to-date information.
