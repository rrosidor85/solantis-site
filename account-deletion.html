Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Solantis: Senior Care & Safety account and all associated data at any time, using either method below.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Solantis app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap App Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and associated data are permanently removed once you confirm.

Option 2 — Request by email

Email robinsonrosidor@gmail.com from the email address on your account, with the subject line "Account Deletion Request." We will verify your identity and delete your account.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • Your account and profile (name, email, phone number)
  • Care recipient profiles and Care Team associations
  • Medications, schedules, adherence history, and vital signs
  • Photos and documents uploaded to the Document Vault
  • All other personal and health data associated with your account

Data we may retain

We may retain a limited set of records where required by law (for example, transaction or tax records related to subscriptions). Any such records are kept only as long as legally required and are not used for any other purpose.

Timing

Account deletion begins immediately upon confirmation. All personal and health data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

Note about subscriptions: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active App Store subscription. To cancel, open Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone (or the App Store > account > Subscriptions), select Solantis, and cancel. Subscriptions are billed and managed by Apple.

Questions

If you need help with account deletion, contact us at robinsonrosidor@gmail.com.
