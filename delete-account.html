Solantis Account Deletion

Request to delete your account and associated data

How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your Solantis account and all associated data, you can do so directly from the app or by contacting us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and will process your request promptly.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the Solantis app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon).
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm your identity and tap "Permanently Delete Account".

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

Send an email to support@solantis.health with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include the email address associated with your Solantis account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Processing Time: Account deletion requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  1. Account information — your name, email address, and profile details
  2. Medication schedules — all medication reminders and tracking history
  3. Health vitals — any recorded health data and vitals logs
  4. Care circle data — your connections with family members and caregivers
  5. App preferences — notification settings and personalization data

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, all associated data will be irretrievably removed from our servers. Please make sure to export any data you wish to keep before requesting deletion.

Data Retention

After account deletion, we may retain certain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you, in accordance with our Privacy Policy. No personally identifiable information will be retained after your deletion request is processed.
